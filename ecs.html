ABOUT

The Environmental Charter School has an emphasis on their student's involvement with nature. The proposed building for a middle school for the ECS expansion is located in Carrick; directly adjacent to the greenery of Phillip's Park. This site needs a unit for urban revitalization, but already offers various amenities to a potential new school. The design of the ECS features a green courtyard that bleeds into Phillip's Park to blend together the built environment to nature's environment.
